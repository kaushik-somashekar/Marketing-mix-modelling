# Project Summary: Marketing Attribution Model

## Objective
Compare multiple attribution models to understand how digital marketing channels contribute to customer conversions and revenue.

## Methodology
1. Generate realistic customer journey touchpoint data.
2. Identify converted customer journeys.
3. Apply first-touch, last-touch, linear, position-based, and time-decay attribution.
4. Allocate conversion credit and revenue to each channel.
5. Calculate ROAS using attributed revenue and media cost.
6. Compare channel performance across models.
7. Translate results into marketing recommendations.

## Business Value
- Better marketing budget allocation
- Improved ROAS measurement
- Reduced over-crediting of last-click channels
- Stronger understanding of assisted conversions
- Better campaign optimization decisions
